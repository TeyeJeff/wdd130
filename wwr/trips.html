    White River Volta

    Ready for an epic challenge? Experience the White Volta River like never before. Join us on a 5-day journey through the Grand Canyon, where you'll paddle Class III to V rapids and camp out surrounded by stunning scenery.

    Duration: 7 days | Difficulty: Advanced | Price: $1,550

    Futaleufu River

    Ready for a family adventure? This 3-day expedition down the roaring rivers of the Rocky Mountains offers the perfect mix of thrilling Class II-IV rapids, scenic mountain views, and camping. It is ideal for families or intermediate rafters looking for an unforgettable experience.

    Duration: 5 days | Difficulty: Intermediate | Price: $900

    Nenana River

    Experience the ultimate thrill on a 2-day rafting expedition. This adventure is perfect for seasoned rafters, challenging your skills with powerful Class IV and V rapids. You'll be led by expert guides and spend a night camping to complete the journey.

    Duration: 3 days | Difficulty: Expert | Price: $1000

    Training

    We have trained guides for 25 years and every year we strive to improve our program and build upon our success. In other words, you will be the recipient of a very comprehensive training program. 15 days to learn the entire range of skills required to be a professional river guide, or just steer your own craft on Class III-IV water.

    Duration: 15 days | Difficulty: Beginner | Price: $2,500
